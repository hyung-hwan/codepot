 |                                                                                  |
 |  INSTALLATION                                                                    |
 |__________________________________________________________________________________|
 
  1)  Place the complete folder "calm-theme-for-websvn" in your "templates"-directory
  
  2)  Open your confi.inc (from "includes" folder) in an editor and look for the line saying:
  
      $config->setTemplatePath("$locwebsvnreal/templates/Standard/");

      and change it into:

      $config->setTemplatePath("$locwebsvnreal/templates/calm-theme-for-websvn/");

  3)  Enjoy.
